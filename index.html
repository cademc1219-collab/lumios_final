Features

Everything you need
to actually learn.

Six study modes, an AI coach, and a community of creators — all in one place.

01

Flashcard flip

Classic tap-to-reveal cards. Swipe at your own pace or let Leo reorder them based on what you need most.

02

Multiple choice

AI generates convincingly wrong answers automatically. Every quiz feels real without any extra work from the creator.

03

Type the answer

Force your brain to retrieve from scratch — harder, but proven to stick far better long-term.

04

Sequence mode

Drag and drop events or steps into the right order. Perfect for timelines, biology cycles, and math processes.

05

Spelling & audio

Leo reads words aloud. Students type what they hear — just like a real spelling test. Built for young learners.

06

Import from anywhere

Paste a YouTube link, URL, PDF, or photo of your notes. Leo reads it and builds your set automatically.
